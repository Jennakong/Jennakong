How I Built a Fashion Brand from Scratch

Behind the scenes of MANIA by Jennifer — from fabric sourcing to campaign launch.

Fashion Business

It started with a sketch. A vision. A need to build something rooted in identity and elevated by design. This is the story of how I built MANIA by Jennifer.

From fabric markets to mood boards, I immersed myself in the craft. I travelled, tested materials, worked with seamstresses and pattern cutters — all before launching the first capsule collection.

But the magic didn’t stop there. Campaigns, branding, and the story behind the clothes mattered just as much as the outfits themselves. I wanted women to feel connected to something deeper when they wore MANIA.

This journey taught me that fashion isn't just style — it's soul, strategy, and storytelling.
